Frequently Asked Questions & Policy

What is Bliss Wellness?

Bliss Wellness is a holistic wellness brand offering intentionally crafted products and embodied wellness services. Our offerings are designed to support nervous system regulation, emotional grounding, and spiritual connection through plant medicine, movement, meditation, and touch.

What types of offerings does Bliss Wellness provide?

We currently offer guided breathwork, intention candles & body care, intuitive guidance, and somatic alchemy sessions, both in private and group formats (as available). These experiences are designed to support embodiment, energetic regulation, trauma-informed release, and deeper mind–body awareness. All services are grounded in nervous-system safety, intuition, and conscious movement.
